## Image Guidelines

- **Format**: PNG (preferred) or SVG for best quality
- **Background**: Transparent background preferred
- **Size**: At least 400px wide for high-resolution displays
- **Orientation**: Horizontal/landscape orientation works best
- **Color**: Can be full color - the website applies a grayscale filter that removes on hover

## Logo Sources

For official logos, contact each organization directly or check their:
- Press/Media pages
- Brand guidelines pages
- Partner resource centers

Many organizations have brand asset portals like:
- Brandfetch (e.g., brandfetch.com/[organization])
- Official press kits
- Partner portals
